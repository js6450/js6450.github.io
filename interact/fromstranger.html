From Stranger, With Love.

From Stranger, With Love, is one of the pieces being created under the theme of formation of human relationships over digital and analog means of communication. FromStrangerWithLove.com provides a digital service that sends someone a postcard. The user is asked to fill out a simple form, including the intended recipient's name and address, the degree of their relationship with the recepient, and the general content of the message. Based on the answers of the users, the content of the postcard is fabricated, hand-written, then delivered through the postal service.
